Sushi Abroad: When your sushi addiction follows you everywhere

Ariyoshi: South Orange, NJ

Based in my hometown of South Orange NJ, this is my go-to everytime I go home. It's ironically my family's go-to every Christmas dinner when we are too lazy to cook a big meal. What do you want for Christmas? Sushi? Done!

Ichi Ichi Ku Izakaya: Melbourne, Australia

A friend of mine was abroad for a year in Melbourne and said this Green Dragon roll was out of this world. Based on reviews online and her recommendation, definitely check this place out if you're ever down unda!

Symphony Sushi: Boston, Massachusetts

My Boston friends rave about this place! I've had this place a couple times when I'm in Boston and it always hits the spot. It's afforable, it's delicious, and it's the number one spot for students. Like most places, get the most bang for your buck with the lunch special :) Pictured here is a salmon combo - what else is new?!
