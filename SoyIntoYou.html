Welcome to 6ixSushi! A sushi specific Toronto food blog.

What's trending this summer? Sushi Burritos

Fish'd By Edo

If you haven't been yet, you must check out the open air food court at Adelaide and Queen. Not only is there Holy Chuck for your burger cravings and the ever so popular rolled ice cream, but also a sushi burrito made my the beloved Toronto favourite, Edo Sushi. I didn't know what to expect, but boy was this delicious. They are using fresh ingredients (wouldn't expect anything less of Edo) and it was the perfect size to not feel uncomfortably full (or as Louis CK would say, eat till you hate yourself). I would absolutely suggest trying this place out for yourself - it's more than just a perfect picture!

Sushitto On the Road

This is a food truck and BEWARE- there will be lines. There's a lot of speculation as to whether it's worth it to wait for this. I've heard great things about them, while others have said it's not quite as fresh as regular sushi. Regardless, must try at least once, especially if they are in your neighbourhood!

Featured restaurants:

Yasu: The Annex

Omasake style, like the famous documentary Jiro dreams of Sushi! This is one of the highest rated sushi restaurants not only in Toronto, but all of Canada and the western hemisphere. It certainly did not disappoint, but you should expect to try some weird stuff. After our meal there, Yasu offered us Cod Testical sushi - yes, you heard me - and my boyfriend and I opted out. Check out the full review here!

Tone Sushi: Queen West

My favorite spicy salmon roll in the city - enough said, right? But seriously, this place is pretty solid. They have very fresh sushi at a fair price, great interior, and one of the best fish/rice ratio I've seen. Check out the full review here!

Fushimi: The Village

This place is my absolute favourite. I would go here every weekend if I didn't need to get a variety of places for this blog! I actually have yet to check out their dinner options, but their lunch prices/selection is awesome. Not to mention the a la carte options - $7 for two rolls and soup & salad! Pinch me! Check out the full review here!
